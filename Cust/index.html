What is interactive media??

Any computer-delivered electronic system that allows the user to control, mix, and alter various forms of media, including as text, sound, video, computer graphics, and animation, is known as interactive media. Computer, memory storage, digital (binary) data, telephone, television, and other information technologies are all integrated into interactive media. Training programmes, video games, electronic encyclopaedias, and travel guides are some of the most prevalent uses. Interactive media, which are considered the next generation of electronic information systems, transform the user's position from observer to participation.
